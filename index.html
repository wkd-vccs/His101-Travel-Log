Journey Through Rome

Welcome to my travel log! I’ve used a time machine to explore five iconic locations that defined Roman life. From the screams in the Colosseum to the silence of a temple, I wanted to experience what made Rome such a notable place. This journey is based on research from ancient Roman texts and modern historians and my goal is to understand how Roman society functioned. Each stop is written as if I’m actually there, describing what I see, smell, hear and feel.
